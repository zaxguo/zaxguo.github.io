I am now a tenure-track Assistant Professor at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in the school of Computer Science. 
My research is in the intersection of OS x Arch x PL, under the umbrella of efficiency and security.   
Prior to UESTC, I was lucky to be awarded [Top Minds](https://career.huawei.com/reccampportal/portal5/topminds.html) from Huawei, where I had a little dabble in industry. 

I received my doctoral degree in 2022 from University of Virginia, advised by Prof. [Felix Xiaozhu Lin](https://fxlin.github.io) and bachelor degree in 2016 from Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Prof. [Zibin Zheng](https://www.zibinzheng.com/).
Prior to UVA, I have spent amazing four years at Purdue University with awesome folks of [XSEL](http://xsel.rocks) in the School of Electric and Computer Engineering. 

My past and ongoing projects span multiple aspects of **kernel**, including **drivers** that provide energy isolation, **file systems** that are highly secure on IoT devices, to **dynamic binary translation** that run a wimpy peripheral cores.
